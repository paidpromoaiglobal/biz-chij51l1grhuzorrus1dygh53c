About Us

Shree Jain Yoga Centre

Shree Jain Yoga Centre is a dedicated wellness establishment nestled in the heart of Rainbow Nagar, Puducherry. We are committed to guiding individuals of all ages and fitness levels on their journey towards physical strength, mental clarity, and spiritual well-being through the timeless practice of yoga. Our centre blends traditional yogic philosophy with modern wellness techniques to offer a truly holistic experience.

Located conveniently at 4th Cross Extension, our centre provides a calm and peaceful space away from the hustle of daily life. Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced practitioner, our expert instructors are here to support your personal growth. We believe in the Jain principles of harmony, discipline, and non-violence, which are deeply woven into every session we offer.

  • Expert and certified yoga instructors with deep knowledge of traditional and modern yoga practices
  • Peaceful and well-equipped studio environment in a convenient Rainbow Nagar location
  • Classes available for all age groups and fitness levels including beginners, seniors, and advanced practitioners
